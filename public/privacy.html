Welcome to MissionGrid, an advanced educational platform dedicated to competitive exam preparation. Your privacy is paramount to us. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, protect, utilize, and manage your personal data when you use our mobile application ("MissionGrid" or "the App") and our services.

By accessing, downloading, or using MissionGrid, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is strictly necessary to provide a personalized, secure, and fully functional learning experience.

Personal Profile Information

  • Full Name: To personalize your dashboard and generated study materials.
  • Mobile Phone Number: Used exclusively for security authentication via OTP validation.
  • Email Address: Optional; collected only if explicitly provided to contact you regarding account recovery or support.

Educational Content & Activity

  • Study Submissions: PDFs, images, practice answer sheets, or test papers uploaded directly by you.
  • Test & Progress Records: Data relating to your performance on quizzes, practice papers, and curriculum tracks.
2

How We Use Information

We use the information collected to fulfill the educational objectives of MissionGrid. Specifically, we utilize your information for:

Secure Authentication: Sending Mobile OTPs to verify your identity, preventing fraud, and ensuring unauthorized users cannot access your profile.

AI-Assisted Assessment: Evaluating your uploaded PDFs, mock sheets, and questions to yield instant, descriptive feedback and corrections.

App Improvement: Analyzing technical parameters to patch bugs, build better design workflows, and boost service response speed.

3

Data Storage & Security

The security of your personal data is vital to us. We execute high-grade digital, administrative, and cloud security methodologies to shield your profiles:

  • Standard Protocols: All communications between the app and backend utilize secure, encrypted HTTPS protocols.
  • Restricted Access: Only system components and authorized operators required for your exam feedback have read/write access privileges.
  • Durable Cloud Framework: Personal data points are secured via strict role-based access rules.
4

Third-Party Services

We strictly do not sell, rent, lease, or distribute your personal info to any advertising agencies or unauthorized third parties.

To enable app infrastructure and stable operation, we engage Google Firebase (provided by Google LLC). Your data is transferred and stored inside Firebase under industry-compliant Data Processing Agreements. The sub-services used are:

Firebase Authentication

Manages identity assertion, phone logins, OTP codes, session tokens, and metadata to lock and secure user profiles.

Cloud Firestore

Stores profile variables, structured names, test scores, dynamic progress indices, and study structures with secure, server-enforced security rules.

Firebase Storage

Secure, cloud-hosted repository for hosting and loading user-uploaded study submissions, image notes, and structural PDFs.

For details regarding how Google manages, logs, and processes information under Firebase, please check the Google Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy
